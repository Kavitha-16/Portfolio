Crop Chain

CropChain is a farmer-centric platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and modern agricultural resources. It provides personal assistance to farmers with limited technical knowledge or language barriers by issuing a unique card similar to an Aadhaar card. This card connects farmers to the CropChain platform, which handles activities like posting crops for sale, accessing agricultural services, and obtaining critical information. The platform simplifies processes, ensuring farmers receive the support they need to improve productivity and profitability.
